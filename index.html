Our Mission

Revolutionizing the Agricultural Sector

Driven by a shared passion, we as an agricultural club aim to bridge the gap between agricultural challenges and engineering innovation by bringing together a community of passionate students who develop solutions for a sustainable and thriving agricultural future. Agriculture being the backbone of our civilization, we aim to motivate, educate, and inculcate in youth our passion for agriculture and to be able to find solutions to various day-to-day problems faced by our farmers. We also host hackathons, site visits, entrepreneurship summits, and agricultural debates to raise awareness and pique students' interest in this niche field. For the future ahead, we plan on taking on even more challenges, growing our family and delving more into research and development for a bright future ahead.
